Ingredients - One-Pot Creamy Lemon Salmon Pasta (4 servings)

Ingredients

  • 1 to 1.5 lbs skinless salmon fillets
  • 12 oz short pasta (penne or rotini)
  • 1 small onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 3 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (or 2 tbsp cream cheese)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 lemon (juice and zest)
  • 2 cups fresh baby spinach
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup water
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
