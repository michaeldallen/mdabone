# One-Pot Creamy Lemon Salmon Pasta
> *Comforting, Rich, and Perfectly Balanced — Family-of-Four Large Print Edition*

| Prep Time | Cook Time | Total Time | Servings |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 10 mins | 20 mins | 30 mins | 4 people |

---

## 🍳 Cooking Steps

### 1. Salmon Preparation
Lay your skinless salmon fillets flat on a cutting board. Slice them into long 1-inch strips, then cut across those strips horizontally to create clean, uniform, bite-sized 1-inch squares (the size of a game die).

### 2. Sear the Salmon
Heat a large tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat in a large pot or heavy skillet. Season salmon cubes with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Sear for 2 minutes to get a light golden color. Remove cubes immediately to a plate; the inside must stay undercooked for now.

### 3. Build Flavor Base
Turn the heat down to medium in the same pot. Add the finely diced onion. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes until soft and translucent, scraping up any flavorful browned bits from the bottom. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for 1 final minute until fragrant.

### 4. Simmer the Pasta
Pour in 3 cups of vegetable broth, 1 cup of water, and your dry short pasta. Bring the liquid to a boil, then turn the heat down to a low simmer. Cover the pot and cook for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking, until the pasta is just shy of al dente.

### 5. Create Creamy Sauce
Keep the heat on low. Stir in ¼ cup of heavy cream (or 2 tbsp cream cheese), the fresh lemon juice, lemon zest, and ¼ cup of grated Parmesan cheese. Stir gently until the cheese completely melts and creates a glossy, uniform sauce over the pasta.

### 6. Steam Finish
Gently fold the seared salmon cubes (and any juices left on the plate) back into the pot, along with 2 cups of fresh baby spinach. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid, **TURN OFF THE HEAT COMPLETELY**, and let it rest for 3 minutes. The trapped residual steam will perfectly flake the salmon and wilt the spinach. Serve hot.

## 🛒 Shopping Checklist
- [ ] 1 to 1.5 lbs Skinless Salmon Fillets
- [ ] 12 oz Short Pasta (Penne or Rotini)
- [ ] 1 small Onion
- [ ] 3 cloves Garlic
- [ ] 3 cups Low-Sodium Vegetable Broth
- [ ] 1/4 cup Heavy Cream (or Cream Cheese)
- [ ] 1/4 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
- [ ] 1/2 unit Lemon (for juice & zest)
- [ ] 2 cups Fresh Baby Spinach
