# Pasta Prep

## 🍳 Cooking Steps

### 1. Cook Pasta & Save Water
Once the water boils, add the spaghetti.

Cook for 9 (to 11) minutes, stirring occasionally, until al dente.  

**CRITICAL STEP: Carefully scoop out and reserve 1 cup of the starchy pasta cooking water**

Drain the remaining pasta in a strainer.
