# Garlic Butter Shrimp Scampi
> *Over Spaghetti with Roasted Broccoli — HelloFresh 4-Person Scale Large Print Edition*

| Prep Time | Cook Time | Total Time | Servings |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 10 mins | 30 mins | 40 mins | 4 people |

---

## 🍳 Cooking Steps

### 1. Oven & Water Prep
Set your oven rack to the top position and preheat the oven to 450°F. Bring a large pot of heavily salted water to a rolling boil. Wash and thoroughly dry all fresh produce.

### 2. Chop & Zest
Cut all broccoli florets into 1-inch bite-sized pieces. Grate the zest of both lemons, then cut the lemons into quarters. Peel and completely mince or grate your 4 garlic cloves.

### 3. Roast Broccoli
Toss the cut broccoli directly on a baking sheet with a generous drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast on the top oven rack until the edges are browned and crispy, about 12-15 minutes.

### 4. Cook Pasta & Save Water
Once the water boils, drop the spaghetti. Cook for 9-11 minutes, stirring occasionally, until al dente. **CRITICAL STEP: Carefully scoop out and reserve ½ cup of the starchy pasta cooking water**, then drain the remaining pasta in a strainer.

### 5. Mix Garlic Butter
While your pasta cooks, soften 3 tablespoons of butter in a small microwave-safe bowl (about 10 seconds). Mix in the lemon zest, half of your Parmesan cheese, a pinch of your minced garlic, and a pinch of chili flakes. Mash with a fork and season with salt and pepper.

### 6. Cook the Shrimp
Rinse the shrimp under cold water and pat them completely dry with paper towels. Toss them in a bowl with olive oil, your remaining minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and cook, stirring occasionally, until fully opaque and pink (2-4 minutes).

### 7. Combine & Serve
To the pan with the cooked shrimp, add your drained spaghetti, roasted broccoli, stock concentrate, the prepared garlic butter, and the saved ½ cup of pasta cooking water. Toss continuously over low heat until everything is coated in a glossy sauce. Finish with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice, remaining parmesan, and remaining chili flakes to taste.
