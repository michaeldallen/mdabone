# Garlic Butter

## 🍳 Cooking Steps

### 1. Zest Lemons
Zest lemons

Cut the lemons into quarters.

### 2. Grate Parmesan
Grate 3 tablespoons of Parmesan cheese.

### 3. Mix Garlic Butter
Soften 6 tablespoons of butter in a small microwave-safe bowl (about 10 seconds). 

Mix in:
* lemon zest
* Parmesan cheese (3 tablespoons)
* a pinch of your minced garlic
* a pinch of chili flakes

Mash with a fork and season with salt and pepper.

## 🛒 Shopping Checklist
- [ ] 2 units Lemon (for zest)
- [ ] 4 cloves Garlic
- [ ] 6 tbsp Parmesan Cheese (use half here)
- [ ] 1 tsp Chili Flakes

## 🧂 Pantry Essentials
- [ ] 3 tbsp Butter
- [ ] Kosher Salt
- [ ] Black Pepper

## 🛠️ Tools Needed
- [ ] Small Microwave Bowl
- [ ] Garlic Press
