# Roasted Broccoli

## 🍳 Cooking Steps

### 1. Chop
Cut all broccoli florets into 1-inch bite-sized pieces.

### 2. Roast Broccoli
Oven:
* Set your oven rack to the top position
* preheat oven to 450°F.

Toss the cut broccoli directly on a baking sheet with a generous drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper. 

Roast 12-15 minutes until the edges are browned and crispy.
