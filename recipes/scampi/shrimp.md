# Shrimp

## 🍳 Cooking Steps

### 1. Prep Shrimp
Rinse the shrimp under cold water and pat them completely dry with paper towels. 

Toss them in a bowl with:
* olive oil
* remaining minced garlic
* salt
* pepper.

### 2. Cook the Shrimp
Heat a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and cook, stirring occasionally, until fully opaque and pink (2-4 minutes).
