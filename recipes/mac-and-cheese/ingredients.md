# Ingredients List For Best Ever Macaroni Cheese (4 servings)

[🖨 Printable version](https://michaeldallen.github.io/mdabone/recipes/mac-and-cheese/ingredients.html)

## 🛒 Ingredients
- [ ] 50g Baguette
- [ ] 350g Spiral or other short pasta
- [ ] 1 clove Garlic
- [ ] 500ml Whole milk
- [ ] 250g Vegetarian mature cheddar
- [ ] 50g Parmesan (or vegetarian alternative)
- [ ] 3 tbsp Butter (2 tbsp + 1 tbsp melted)
- [ ] 1 tsp English mustard powder
- [ ] 3 tbsp Plain flour
- [ ] Salt & pepper
